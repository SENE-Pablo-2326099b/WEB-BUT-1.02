MOODIIFY

Because you deserve to listen to the music that describes you.

This website was made as a student project for the course "Web Development" at the University of Aix-en-Provence. It was made by a team of 4 students:
Baurens-Torrente Valentin
Orlandi Lenny
Griguer Nathan
Sene Pablo
The idea come from the fact that we all love music and we wanted to create a website that could help people discover new music based on their mood. So we decided to first put this idea to a concept on Figma and then after that we started to code the website. And by all putting or sweat and blood into this project we finally made it and we are proud of it. so enjoy this website and discover new music. the MOODIIFY team.
